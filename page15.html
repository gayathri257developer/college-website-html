About Us

Krupanidhi Degree College provides education to empower the students to recognize and optimize their full potential to achieve excellence in academic work and develop highly skilled human resources with the perspective to enable them to adapt to an intellectually and technologically changing environment. The institution imparts quality education and helps students to equip themselves with creative, analytical and academic abilities & managerial qualities which will enable them to a successful career. The college is located in a very beautiful eco friendly environment which provides students a serene and calm atmosphere ideal for learning. The college was established in the year 2001 with the main objectives of developing quality citizens through education and training and to raise the dignity of teaching profession by visionary educationalist Prof. Dr. Suresh Nagpal. The College has all along emphasized the holistic approach and total personality development of pupils, through educational and training programmes undertaken on the basis of service and providing dynamic leadership. This college is studded with a band of dynamic, intellectual, committed teaching faculty ably supported by the equally ready office staff. The dedicated faculty is lead by the foresighted and vibrant Principal. This institution is built on a strong foundation to cater to the ever changing requirements of the students of "GENZEE". The Degree College Uniquely stands out in choosing the Modern Gurukula system by imparting knowledge to make every student a fully fledged learned person.
