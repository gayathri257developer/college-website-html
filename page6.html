B.Com is an undergraduate degree in commerce and related subjects. The Bachelor of Commerce degree is designed to provide the student with a wide range of managerial skills while at the same time building competence in particular areas of business studies; where students are exposed to general business principles, taking courses in accounting/finance, auditing, income tax, statistics, marketing, economics, and information systems.
