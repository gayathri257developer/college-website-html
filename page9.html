The BA courses give ample exposure to the students in their chosen subjects. The course is designed to familiarize the students with the basics in each subject so that they can understand, analyze and pursue a subject of their choice further. Seminars and workshops are organized by the Department to keep the students abreast with the latest development in the various fields. Students are encouraged to do research and publish papers and attend conferences organized by various colleges. Students participate in debates, presentations and wall magazine writing. They are encouraged to explore areas of interest in their chosen subjects and apply it to the society the live in.
